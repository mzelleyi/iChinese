iHuayu

iHuayu 讲华语（商用） application is developed by the Speak Mandarin Campaign as a handy resource on business terminology.

  
 
 
 
 

Speak Mandarin Campaign

The Speak Mandarin Campaign (SMC) is driven by the Promote Mandarin Council. Its objective is to encourage Chinese Singaporeans to use Mandarin in their daily lives and cultivate an appreciation for Chinese culture and language.
 

Partners
Speak Mandarin Campaign thanks the following partners for their strong support for this app:
 
 
 
 

Singapore Workforce Development Agency

The Singapore Workforce Development Agency (WDA) enhances the competitiveness of our workforce by encouraging workers to learn for life and advance with skills. In today's economy, most jobs require not just knowledge, but also skills. WDA collaborates with employers, industry associations, the Union and training organisations, to develop and strengthen the Continuing Education and Training system that is skills-based, open and accessible, as a mainstream pathway for all workers - young and older, from rank and file to professionals and executives - to upgrade and advance in their careers and lives. For more information, please visit http://www.wda.gov.sg
 
 
 
 

Media Development Authority

The Media Development Authority of Singapore (www.mda.gov.sg) promotes the growth of globally competitive Animation, Broadcast, Film, Games, Interactive Media, Music and Publishing industries. It also regulates the media sector to safeguard the interest of consumers and promotes a connected society.

 
   

Translation Standardization Committee

The Translation Standardisation Committee for the Chinese Media was set up in 1990 for the purpose of standardising Chinese translations of commonly used terms in Singapore. For more information, please visit http://chineseterms.zaobao.com.sg/
 
 
 
 

GistXL Technology Pte Ltd

GistXL Technology Pte Ltd develops, customizes and manages software and services that allow governments, corporations and individuals to overcome language barriers and reap the full advantages of globalization. Its assistive technologies help language learners and the visually impaired acquire and use language through transforming text data into usable formats, through audio-enablement, romanization, translation, part-of-speech tagging, etc. For more information, please visit http://www.gistxl.com
